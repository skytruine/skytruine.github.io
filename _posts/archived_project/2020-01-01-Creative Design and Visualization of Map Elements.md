# 1. Introduction
&emsp;I have tried a variety of different methods of using map elements to design: ①use the map patch for graphic design; ②hand-drawn map; ③cool map visualization effect. One of the things that made me most proud was that I made a theme poster for a large geographic information conference, which was praised by lots of the participants. Moreover, in the world book day poster design competition that I participated in by the same method, I stood out among more than 13,000 people and won the first prize (less than 30 people).
