&emsp;One of my hobbies helpful to scientific research and cartography is graphic design.I have been trained as a professional UI designer for 8 months and obtained the certificate of graphic designer. So far, I have been a full-time graphic designer intern for one month and an amateur graphic designer for nearly four years, and have done nearly a hundred large and small design projects.Long-term study and practice of design have brought me a leap in technology and aesthetics, and these abilities have brought me great advantages in the work related to cartography and system design.
